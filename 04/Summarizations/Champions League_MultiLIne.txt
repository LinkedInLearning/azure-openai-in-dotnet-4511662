The Champions League is a sports competition primarily known for association football, 
where top-performing club teams from national competitions compete. 
While most often associated with the UEFA Champions League, 
there are similar competitions in various regions, and for women's football 
and futsal as well. Besides football, there are Champions Leagues in other sports 
including volleyball, handball, nine-pin bowling, table tennis, ice hockey, 
darts, water polo, basketball, cricket, and track cycling.
