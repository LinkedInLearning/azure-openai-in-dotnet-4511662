The Super Bowl is the annual championship game of the National Football League (NFL), played on the second Sunday in February since 2022, and has been the final game of every NFL season since 1966. It was created as part of a 1966 merger agreement between the NFL and the American Football League (AFL) and is among the world's most-watched single sporting events.
