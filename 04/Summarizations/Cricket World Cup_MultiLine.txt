The Cricket World Cup, also known as ICC Men's Cricket World Cup, 
is the international championship of One Day International (ODI) cricket, 
organized every four years by the International Cricket Council (ICC). 
The event, one of the most viewed worldwide, includes a qualification phase 
leading up to a finals tournament with teams from around the globe. 
The current champions are England, who won in 2019, and the 2023 World Cup is ongoing in India,
